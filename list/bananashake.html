Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

It is so refreshing and it's sweet and tasty

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Directions

  1. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender;
    blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 335 calories; protein 12.8g; carbohydrates 34.1g;
fat 18.8g; cholesterol 9.8mg; sodium 202.8mg.
